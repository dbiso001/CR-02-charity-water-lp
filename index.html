Why water — and why now

663 million people live without clean water. For students, that number is more than a statistic — it’s a chance to act. Clean water radically improves health, education, and economic opportunity.

Your donation funds community-led projects. You’ll receive updates and can trace exactly where your impact flows. That’s transparency — and it’s the heart of this campaign.

What your gift unlocks

  • Health: fewer water-borne illnesses and clinic visits
  • Education: students spend more time in class, not collecting water
  • Opportunity: communities build, grow, and thrive
